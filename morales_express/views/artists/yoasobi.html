Yoasobi

Yoasobi is a Japanese pop duo formed in 2019, consisting of musician and record producer Ayase and singer-songwriter Lilas Ikuta, who performs under the moniker Ikura. The duo is known for their unique concept of turning fictional stories into music, with the slogan "novel into music." They originally released songs based on selected stories posted on Monogatary.com, a social media website for creative writing operated by Sony Music Entertainment Japan
